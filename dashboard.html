    Why We Need to Donate Food

    Food donation is an impactful way to fight hunger and reduce food waste. Every donation helps a family in need, spreading hope and care in the community. Together, we can create a more compassionate and equitable world. Here are some statistics on food waste in India: Amount of food wasted In 2022-23, India wasted 74 million tonnes of food, which is 22% of the country's foodgrain output. This is second only to China in terms of household food waste. Per capita food waste The UN estimates that the average Indian person wastes 55 kg of food per year.
